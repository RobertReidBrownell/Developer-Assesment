Architectual Polished System

Diamatic and industry leaders have developed a fusion of polished concrete technologies to create ULTRAFLOR® Architectural Polished Concrete System, a superior, performance-driven concrete floor that is resilient, easy to maintain and very durable for outstanding longevity.
